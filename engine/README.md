# Divini Growth by Divini Group Revenue Engine

Goal: generate the fastest ethical $3,000 by moving high-fit medspas, aesthetic clinics, wellness clinics, and high-ticket salons through personalized outreach, a free Missed Revenue Audit, a $19 Deep Revenue Growth Audit, and a setup or monthly service offer.

Brand position: **Divini Growth by Divini Group** is the front-facing offer for beauty, wellness, medspa, salon, tanning, and clinic operations. Use `divinigrowth.com` for the funnel and `divinigroup@gmail.com` for notifications and replies.

## Funnel Offers

1. **Missed Revenue Audit**: free opt-in audit
2. **Deep Revenue Growth Audit**: $19 paid audit
3. **Revenue Recovery Setup**: $750 first setup, then $1,000 after proof
4. **Monthly Growth Ops Management**: starts at $297 per month

The setup helps clinics respond faster to new leads, follow up with consults, reactivate past clients, improve review flow, and stop losing revenue from manual inbox, text, booking, and DM gaps.

## Revenue Math

- 4 setup clients at $750 = $3,000
- 3 setup clients at $1,000 = $3,000
- 2 setup clients at $750 plus 5 monthly clients at $297 = $2,985 recurring base
- 158 paid audits at $19 = $3,002, but the real purpose of the $19 audit is buyer qualification

Recommended starting path: use the $19 audit to qualify prospects, then sell the first 2 setup clients at $750. Raise to $1,000 once there is proof or testimonial language.

## Compliance Boundaries

- Do not claim medical outcomes.
- Do not automate clinical advice.
- Do not send SMS campaigns without proper consent.
- Do not request or store patient names, medical details, appointment records, treatment notes, screenshots with patient data, or protected health information.
- Position this as admin, lead response, rebooking, reviews, reactivation, and operational follow-up.

## Launch Steps

1. Create the Stripe $19 Payment Link.
2. Run `funnel/create_divini_growth_engine.gs` from `divinigroup@gmail.com`.
3. Paste the free form URL into `googleFormUrl` in `funnel/script.js`.
4. Paste the Stripe Payment Link into `stripeDeepAuditUrl` in `funnel/script.js`.
5. Set the Stripe success URL to the paid intake Google Form.
6. Point `divinigrowth.com` to the landing page host.
7. Start outreach from `outreach_scripts.md`.
