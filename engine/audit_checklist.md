# Free 10-Minute Audit Checklist

Use this on a prospect's website, booking page, social profiles, and public contact flow. Do not enter private systems without permission.

## Lead Capture

- Is there a clear book-now button?
- Is there a contact form?
- Is there a phone number above the fold?
- Is there SMS/chat/DM as an obvious path?
- Are high-value services easy to request?

## Response Risk

- Does the site imply manual follow-up?
- Are inquiries split across phone, form, email, Instagram, Facebook, and booking tools?
- Is there no visible expectation for response time?
- Are there multiple locations or service lines that may route leads inconsistently?

## Consult Conversion

- Are consults free, paid, virtual, or in-person?
- Is there an obvious pre-consult intake?
- Is there follow-up after a consult that does not book?
- Is there follow-up after an incomplete form or abandoned booking?

## Recurring Revenue

- Do they offer memberships, packages, GLP-1, hormones, peptides, IV therapy, tanning packages, facials, injectables, lasers, hair removal, or body contouring?
- Do they need rebooking reminders?
- Do they need package renewal reminders?
- Do they need post-service review/referral requests?

## What To Recommend

Pick 1-3 only:

- Missed-lead tracker
- Contact form to sheet/CRM
- First-response template
- Second-touch follow-up template
- No-book consult follow-up
- Rebooking reminder workflow
- Membership/package renewal tracker
- Google review request workflow
- Staff SOP for daily follow-up queue

## Paid Sprint Scope

A $750 starter sprint should include only what can be delivered in 3-5 business days:

- one lead source
- one tracker
- three to five templates
- one light automation if access/tools allow
- one SOP
- one check-in

Anything beyond that becomes a phase 2 upsell.
