# Divini Growth Full Funnel Engine

## Goal

Create the fastest ethical path to $3,000 by moving high-fit medspas, aesthetic clinics, wellness clinics, and high-ticket salons through:

1. Personalized outreach
2. Free Missed Revenue Audit
3. $19 Deep Revenue Growth Audit
4. Service offer
5. Monthly fulfillment

## Why These Niches First

Priority order:

1. Medspas and aesthetic clinics
2. Wellness, functional, concierge, and longevity clinics
3. High-ticket salons with memberships, bridal, extensions, color, or packages
4. Tanning salons with memberships or packages

Reasoning:

- Medspas and wellness clinics sell higher-ticket services, which makes a missed consult easier to connect to revenue.
- They often receive leads from multiple places: website, calls, DMs, ads, booking tools, and referrals.
- A fast lead response and follow-up system can feel immediately useful to the owner or manager.
- Traditional salons can still work, but the fastest $3,000 path is usually higher-ticket consult-heavy businesses.

Market support:

- AmSpa reports the medical spa industry has passed $17B in revenue and is growing by more than $1B per year.
- Grand View Research projects the global medical spa market at 15.9% CAGR from 2026 to 2033.
- Global Wellness Institute reports the wellness economy reached $6.8T in 2024 after 7.9% annual growth.

## Funnel Stages

### Stage 1: Research

For each prospect, scan:

- Website homepage
- Booking page
- Contact page
- Instagram bio
- Recent Instagram posts
- Google Business Profile if available

Capture one real observation before outreach. Examples:

- They promote consult-based services but the booking link is hard to find.
- They ask people to DM but there is no obvious response expectation.
- They offer packages or memberships but do not show a follow-up path.
- They have strong reviews but no visible rebooking or review capture flow.
- Their contact form has no next-step expectation.

### Stage 2: Outreach

Channels:

- Email first
- Instagram DM second
- SMS only after lawful consent, existing relationship, or clear opt-in

Daily target:

- 25 researched prospects
- 15 emails
- 10 Instagram DMs
- 10 follow-ups
- 3 free audit submissions
- 1 paid audit buyer every 1-2 days once traffic starts

### Stage 3: Free Missed Revenue Audit

Purpose:

- Build trust
- Show one or two specific gaps
- Avoid giving away the entire solution
- Move qualified businesses into the $19 Deep Revenue Growth Audit

Deliverable:

- 2-3 quick findings
- One simple fix they can use
- Invitation to the $19 audit

### Stage 4: $19 Deep Revenue Growth Audit

Purpose:

- Convert a casual lead into a buyer
- Get better information
- Make the service recommendation feel earned

Deliverable:

- Lead response score
- Booking flow score
- Reactivation score
- Review flow score
- Top 3 missed revenue leaks
- Recommended setup or monthly service

### Stage 5: Service Offer

Fastest path to $3,000:

- 4 clients at $750 setup
- 3 clients at $1,000 setup
- 2 setup clients plus 3 monthly clients

Recommended first offer:

**Revenue Recovery Setup**

- $750 setup for first 2 clients
- Then $1,000 setup
- Optional monthly management from $297 to $750 per month

Recommended monthly menu:

- Lead response and missed inquiry tracker
- Consult follow-up templates
- Reactivation campaign
- Review request flow
- Monthly revenue leak report

## Compliance Boundaries

- Do not request patient names.
- Do not request medical histories.
- Do not request protected health information.
- Do not provide clinical advice.
- Do not make medical outcome claims.
- Do not send automated SMS without consent.
- Honor opt-outs immediately.
- Keep this positioned as business operations, lead response, admin workflow, booking follow-up, and client experience.
