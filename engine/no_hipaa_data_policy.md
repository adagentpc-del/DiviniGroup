# No HIPAA Data Policy

Divini Growth should not request, collect, store, process, or review patient health information for this funnel.

## Do Not Collect

- Patient names
- Patient phone numbers
- Patient emails
- Appointment details tied to a patient
- Medical history
- Treatment notes
- Prescriptions
- Diagnoses
- Photos of patients
- Screenshots containing patient information
- CRM exports with patient records

## Allowed Information

- Business name
- Owner or manager name
- Business email
- Business phone
- Website
- Booking link
- Public social links
- Public service menu
- General business workflow notes
- Tool names
- Anonymous process descriptions

## Form Disclaimer

Use this language on forms:

Do not submit patient names, medical details, treatment information, appointment records, screenshots with patient data, or protected health information. This audit only reviews business operations, lead response, booking flow, and follow-up process.

## If Someone Sends Sensitive Information

1. Do not paste it into AI tools.
2. Do not forward it unless required for deletion or containment.
3. Delete the submission if possible.
4. Ask the sender to resubmit with patient information removed.
5. Log that the item was rejected because it contained protected information.
