# Outreach and Reply Playbook

## Voice Rules

- Sound like a real person.
- Use one specific observation from their site or social page.
- Keep messages short.
- Do not use em dashes.
- Do not hype AI.
- Do not shame their business.
- Do not mention patient data.
- Lead with usefulness and trust.
