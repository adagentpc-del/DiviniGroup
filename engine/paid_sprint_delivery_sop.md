# Revenue Recovery Setup Delivery SOP

Use this after a client buys the $19 Deep Revenue Growth Audit and says yes to setup or monthly management.

## Intake

Ask for:

- Primary website
- Booking link
- Contact form path
- Main inbox or forwarding address
- Current booking/CRM/tool stack
- Staff member who handles leads
- Services they most want to sell
- Current follow-up process
- Approved tone for client-facing messages

Do not ask for patient names, medical details, appointment records, screenshots with patient data, or protected health information.

## Access

Only request what is needed.

Prefer:

- Shared Google Sheet
- Forwarded form notifications
- Screenshots with patient data removed
- Temporary limited access

Reject patient health details. This Divini Growth funnel reviews business operations only.

## Build

Minimum deliverables:

1. Lead source map
2. Missed-lead tracker
3. New inquiry response template
4. No-book consult follow-up template
5. Rebooking or membership follow-up template
6. Review request or reactivation workflow
7. Daily staff SOP
8. Optional automation if simple and safe

## Monthly Management

Monthly deliverables can include:

- Weekly follow-up queue review
- Monthly missed revenue report
- Lead response template updates
- Reactivation campaign support
- Review request workflow support
- Staff SOP improvements
- Monthly strategy call or async summary

## Handoff

Deliver:

- Tracker link or file
- Templates
- SOP
- Short walkthrough script or written walkthrough
- 7-day check-in date

## Upsell Paths

- Additional locations
- More lead sources
- Review request workflow
- Membership/package renewal workflow
- Monthly follow-up dashboard
- CRM cleanup
- Landing page/contact form cleanup
- Staff training
- Monthly Growth Ops Management

## Completion Message

The first version of your follow-up system is set up. The highest-leverage habit now is checking the follow-up queue once per day and making sure every new inquiry gets a second touch if they do not book.

I will check back in 7 days to see what is working, what is being skipped, and what should be tightened.
